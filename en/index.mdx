# Welcome to Snorbe

**Snorbe** is a graph AI-powered research platform for manufacturing industries. It accumulates the latest news, papers, patents, government reports, and internal PDF/Excel/reports as a knowledge graph, automatically detecting unexplored areas (white spaces).

## Three Key Deliverables from Snorbe

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card
    title="Auto White Space Detection"
    icon="sitemap"
  >
    Visualize research density with a knowledge graph integrating public data and internal documents. Highlight sparse areas and suggest themes, companies, and patents to explore next.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Viewpoint Matrix in 30 Seconds"
    icon="table"
  >
    AI generates viewpoint candidates and auto-extracts materials, methods, costs, regulations, and market applications. Export instantly to CSV/Excel for sharing and reporting.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Self-Host & Reuse"
    icon="shield"
  >
    Deploy in your own environment with Docker. Control access per workspace and reuse past research in future projects.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Use

### Just Enter a Theme, Get Reports and Matrices in 10 Minutes

Enter your research theme and optional viewpoint hints, and Snorbe will plan optimally, automatically completing research, extraction, and visualization.

<CardGroup cols={4}>
  <Card title="Theme Input" icon="bullseye">
    Set objectives and viewpoint hints
  </Card>
  <Card title="Autonomous Research Agent" icon="robot">
    Collect latest information via SERP + scraping
  </Card>
  <Card title="Knowledge Graph Creation" icon="diagram-project">
    Organize papers and proposals into nodes/relations
  </Card>
  <Card title="White Space Detection" icon="traffic-light">
    Auto-highlight sparse areas
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<CardGroup cols={4}>
  <Card title="Viewpoint Matrix" icon="chart-simple">
    Export to CSV/Excel in 30 seconds
  </Card>
  <Card title="Structured Report" icon="file-lines">
    Output Markdown/PDF in 10 minutes
  </Card>
  <Card title="Self-Host" icon="lock">
    Deploy internally with Docker Compose
  </Card>
  <Card title="Workspace Sharing" icon="users">
    Manage access rights and history
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Navigate the Graph, Verify Facts, and Decide Next Steps

Integrate public information and internal files into a unified knowledge graph, simultaneously reflecting in viewpoint matrices and reports. Ask follow-up questions or re-crawl via chat, making decisions with visible research history.

<Note>
**Supported Data Formats**: PDF / Excel / PowerPoint
</Note>
